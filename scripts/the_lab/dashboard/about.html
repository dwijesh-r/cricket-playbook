🌹 About Statsledge

The story behind the most comprehensive IPL analytics platform. Where data meets cricket expertise.

📖 What We're Building

Statsledge is a data-driven IPL pre-season magazine: the kind of premium content that coaches reference, commentators cite, and serious cricket analysts bookmark.

We're building the analytics infrastructure to produce:

  • Predicted Playing XIs for all 10 IPL franchises
  • Position-by-position Depth Charts with ratings
  • Player archetype classifications and matchup analysis
  • Tactical insights validated by cricket domain experts
  • The Film Room: NL-powered SQL search across 2.38M ball-by-ball records
  • Pressure analytics with context-aware entry metrics

This isn't about betting or fantasy. It's about experiencing cricket deeper, giving fans insights that enhance how they watch, discuss, and understand the game.

🤔 Why We're Building It

Cricket is more than a sport in India. It's a passion. But most fans consume the game through surface-level statistics. They deserve better.

  • Too shallow: Basic stats, no real insight
  • Too academic: Methodologies nobody can apply
  • Too generic: Not team-specific or context-aware

✓ We're building data-driven analysis that empowers fans, uncovering insights that enhance how they watch, discuss, and understand cricket.
